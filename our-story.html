Our Story

Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR)

PT NOHYOGHI BERKAH SEJAHTERA is committed to creating a positive impact, not only through our natural herbal products but also through our corporate social responsibility (CSR) programs.

Our CSR Initiatives:

  • Education Support for Employees: Providing opportunities for employees to pursue higher education to improve their quality of life.
  • Job Opportunities for Students: Offering part-time jobs for students to support tuition fees and personal development.
  • Community Care: Collaborating with local foundations to distribute health packages and free herbal drinks to families in need.
  • Local Farmer Empowerment: Partnering with Indonesian spice farmers to increase the value of their harvests and support the local economy.
